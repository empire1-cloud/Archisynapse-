- 2.0% (up to $10K/mo)

- Customer processing $50K/mo at 1.5% = $750/mo in fees

### 3. White-Label Licensing (10% of revenue)

- Custom solutions for banks and growing fintechs
- Per-deployment fee: $5K–$50K (one-time)
- Monthly recurring: 3–5% of partner's transaction volume

### 4. Add-ons & Premium Services (5% of revenue)

- Advanced analytics & reporting: +$49/mo
- Custom compliance certifications: +$99/mo
- API access tier upgrades: +$29/mo
- Premium support (24/7 priority): +$199/mo

## Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**CAC Calculation:**
```
Sales & Marketing Spend / New Customers Acquired = CAC

Year 1: $480K / 6,400 customers = $75 CAC
```

**CAC Breakdown:**
- Product Hunt campaign: $10K (high-impact)
- Content marketing: $50K annually
- Paid acquisition (Google, LinkedIn): $20K/mo
- Partner referrals: Commission-based (no upfront cost)

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**LTV Calculation (5-year horizon):**
```
Average Monthly Revenue per User × Gross Margin × Customer Lifespan - CAC

ARPU: $150/mo (blended across all tiers)
Gross Margin: 80% (SaaS model with minimal COGS)
Churn: 5% monthly (95% retention)
Expected Lifespan: 20 months (1 / 0.05)

LTV = ($150 × 0.80 × 20) - $75 = $2,400 - $75 = $2,325
```

**Payback Period:**
```
CAC / (ARPU × Gross Margin) = $75 / ($150 × 0.80) = 0.625 months ≈ 2.4 months
```

### LTV/CAC Ratio

```
LTV / CAC = $2,400 / $75 = 32x
```

**Benchmark**: Healthy SaaS companies maintain 3:1 to 5:1 ratios. Our 32x indicates strong product-market fit potential.

## Key Financial Metrics

### Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Growth

**Target Growth Rate:**
- Months 1-3: 50% MoM
- Months 4-6: 40% MoM
- Months 7-12: 25% MoM
- Months 13-18: 15% MoM

### Net Revenue Retention (NRR)

```
NRR = (Beginning MRR + New MRR + Expansion MRR - Churn MRR) / Beginning MRR
```

**Target:** >110% (expansion revenue exceeds churn)

### Burn Rate & Runway

**Monthly Burn (Year 1):**
```
Operating Expenses - Revenue = Monthly Burn
$100K - $15K (avg Year 1) = $85K burn/mo
```

**Runway with $1.2M seed:**
```
$1.2M / $85K = ~14 months
```

By Month 12 ($50K MRR), runway extends significantly. By Month 20, cash flow positive.

## Sensitivity Analysis

### What if churn increases to 8%?

```
LTV = ($150 × 0.80 × 12.5) - $75 = $1,425
LTV/CAC = $1,425 / $75 = 19x (still healthy but concerning)
```

**Mitigation**: Invest in customer success, feature roadmap alignment.

### What if CAC doubles to $150?

```
Payback Period = $150 / ($150 × 0.80) = 1.25 months (still acceptable)
LTV/CAC = $2,400 / $150 = 16x (still strong)
```

**Mitigation**: Optimize marketing spend, increase referral efficiency.

### What if ARPU drops to $100?

```
LTV = ($100 × 0.80 × 20) - $75 = $1,525
LTV/CAC = $1,525 / $75 = 20.3x (still healthy)
```

**Mitigation**: Improve upsell strategy, increase average transaction volume.
